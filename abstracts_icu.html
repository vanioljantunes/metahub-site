UTI dos Abstracts

The goal of Abstract ICU is simple: review your abstract live and show, in practice, what actually increases your chances of acceptance at international conferences.

It is a monthly live session where you can receive real-time feedback on your abstracts. During the meeting, we analyze the structure, clarity, and scientific language of each abstract and revise it together.

No theory.

Just clear feedback on structure, clarity, and the common mistakes that lead to rejection.
