MetaWriters is the specialized service of the MetaHub platform dedicated to the technical, methodological, and editorial review of scientific manuscripts, with a focus on systematic reviews and meta-analyses.

Our team is composed of trained researchers with strong expertise in scientific methodology and international publication standards. You submit the manuscript and, within up to 7 days, receive the revised version with corrections to content, structure, and formatting, along with constructive and actionable feedback.

MetaWriters — Expert Review

Your dedicated partners in enhancing your research projects

Thank you for your interest in having your article reviewed by one of our experts, the MetaWriters. We are here to improve and refine your articles for a smooth journal submission.

What we offer
  • Review and correction — Your ideas are exceptional, but conveying them effectively is an art. MetaWriters enhance your writing, ensuring your innovative ideas shine with clarity and precision.
  • Structural precision — Our meticulous attention to detail covers every aspect, from title pages to tables, ensuring your paper not only meets but exceeds expected standards.
  • Content excellence — Let us transform your article into a masterpiece: an abstract that highlights your strengths, a discussion connected to your results, and a compelling conclusion.
What we do NOT do
  • Writing the article — Your voice, your words. Submit your complete article and we will refine it.
  • Subject matter expertise — While we are not specialists in your field, our expertise lies in the art of effective communication.
  • Journal-specific formatting — We focus on general, well-organized formatting in terms of structure, content, and grammar. Formatting for your target journal is your responsibility.
Exclusive MetaHub benefits

The MetaWriter service is a courtesy included in your annual MetaHub subscription — no extra cost! Check the checklist in the next steps to ensure you have provided all the necessary information and submit your article now.

Thank you for choosing MetaWriters. We look forward to enhancing your work and contributing to your academic success.

Pre-submission checklist
Abbreviations: full form on first mention, abbreviated thereafter
Consistent formatting: Arial or Times New Roman, size 12
Results format: (OR 1.5; 95% CI 1.2 to 1.8; p = 0.03; I² = 25%)
Non-proper nouns in lowercase: "acetaminophen" not "Acetaminophen"
